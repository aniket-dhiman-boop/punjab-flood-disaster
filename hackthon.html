Safety Precautions

Before Flood

  • Monitor IMD alerts.
  • Store documents in waterproof bags.
  • Keep 72-hr emergency kit ready.

During Flood

  • Avoid walking/driving in water.
  • Turn off gas & electricity.
  • Stay on higher ground.

After Flood

  • Avoid contaminated food/water.
  • Check home safety before entry.
  • Support community recovery.
