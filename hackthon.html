Punjab Flood Disaster 2025

Raising Awareness, Recording Impact, and Promoting Safety

Welcome to the Punjab Flood Awareness Site

The 2025 floods in Punjab, caused by excessive monsoon rains and river overflows, devastated communities across 29 districts. This site records the impact, analyzes the data, shows contributions from leaders and celebrities, and provides safety tips.
